# Operational Language Guide

## Voice

Velkor should sound like engineers who execute infrastructure work daily: direct, measured, practical, and comfortable naming constraints.

## Use

- "Se identificaron cuentas compartidas y se priorizó MFA antes de abrir VPN."
- "Se dejó excepción temporal con responsable y fecha de retiro."
- "Se documentó rollback antes de tocar producción."
- "Separamos lo urgente de lo deseable antes de proponer alcance."

## Avoid

- Generic transformation language.
- Fake certainty or perfect outcomes.
- Partner/certification claims unless verifiable and legally safe.
- "Gratis" as the main conversion promise.
- Startup-style language that hides operational tradeoffs.

## Copy Pattern

1. Name the real environment constraint.
2. Explain the decision made.
3. State the operational artifact left behind.
4. Keep the tone consultive, not promotional.
